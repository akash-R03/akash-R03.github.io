About

Bharath Textile Engineering, an ISO 9001: 2015 certified company, was founded in 1973 and are manufacturers of precision components for Automobile, Pump and Textile Industries. We aim to provide a wide range of components by understanding the requirements of the customers, thus serving and fabricating our range accordingly. Our intensive industry experience and know-how has helped us carve a niche for ourselves in the domestic market. Having established a strong image in India, we look forward to placing our foot in the international market in the nearby future. Our rigorous efforts have strengthened our relationship with existing clients while drawing new clients.

Quality

Our main aim is to produce high quality components complying with international standards.
